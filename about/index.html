About HowToPwn

Building a safer digital world through expertise, innovation, and collaboration.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, HowToPwn emerged from a passionate group of cybersecurity enthusiasts who shared a common vision: to make the digital world more secure through knowledge sharing and practical expertise.

What started as a CTF team quickly evolved into a comprehensive cybersecurity organization, combining competitive success with professional consulting services to deliver real-world value to our clients.

Today, we're proud to be one of Vietnam's leading cybersecurity teams, with a track record of success in both CTF competitions and professional security assessments.

Our Mission

To empower organizations and individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to protect their digital assets while fostering a community of security-minded professionals.
